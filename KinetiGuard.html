Our Innovation

KinetiGuard is a low-cost smart knee brace system that uses IMU sensors and Edge AI technology to detect high-risk knee movements in real time and intervene instantly, preventing sports injuries in young athletes.

Our product is designed for school sports teams and youth sports clubs, helping athletes identify and correct harmful movement patterns during training and competition, reducing the risk of ACL (anterior cruciate ligament) injuries.

How It Works

Real-Time Monitoring

Dual IMU sensors capture knee movement data in real time.

Intelligent Detection

An embedded AI model analyzes movement risk levels instantly on the device.

Immediate Intervention

  • Haptic Alert: Tactile warning within 0.08 seconds
  • Airbag Support: Inflatable pad for physical stabilization

Continuous Learning

Collect anonymous motion data to improve detection accuracy.

Key Features

Edge AI Processing

No phone or cloud required—real-time analysis with zero latency.

Dual-Sensor Fusion

Improves motion recognition accuracy and reduces false alarms.

Scalable Design

Supports team-wide deployment and is adaptable to various sports.

Intended Impact

The Problem

Knee injuries among teens—especially ACL tears—are rising, with higher risk for female athletes.

School sports budgets are limited (typically ¥50–200 per student annually).

Existing solutions are either too expensive (imported braces cost ¥3,000–5,000) or passive/ineffective.

Real-Time Protection

Feedback and support at the moment of risk, not after the fact.

Affordable

Unit cost around ¥235—about one-tenth the price of imported braces.

Easy to Adopt

No need for continuous professional supervision, ideal for school teams.

Quantifiable Impact

A single ACL surgery costs ¥25,000–50,000, with 6–9 months of rehabilitation.

KinetiGuard significantly reduces injury risk, easing financial and emotional strain on families.

Designed for the realities of school sports in China, helping more youth participate safely.

Our Motivation

Having seen peers sidelined by sports injuries—sometimes ending their athletic and academic pursuits—we recognized the gap between existing protective solutions and what schools can actually afford and use. We’re driven to make real-time, intelligent protection accessible to every student athlete.
